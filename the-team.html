The Team

The people who make the magic happen.

At Revel, our team isn't just a lineup of talent. We're a collective of artists, creatives, and storytellers with each member bringing a distinct voice to the experience, united by a shared obsession with creating unforgettable moments.

Jigar is the creative force behind Revel Entertainment, with more than 15 years in live events and entertainment. He began DJing in high school from 2003-2007 during his viral YouTube remix years under the name DJ Jigga, then adopted the name DJ Jig in 2010 as he moved into weddings, clubs, and private celebrations. Before launching Revel in 2016, Jigar worked with two established DJ companies and spent two years as a resident Bollywood DJ. With well over 300 events across his career, he brings a rare blend of creative instinct and operational precision shaped by his background in software engineering and hospitality management. For clients, Jigar delivers seamless open-format DJing, confident MC leadership, and premium production that keep every moment intentional, energetic, and unforgettable.

MC Maulin is a high-energy, detail-driven Master of Ceremonies trusted by top planners to bring clarity, confidence, and momentum to every celebration. Acting as far more than just a voice on the microphone, he serves as the definitive guide for the timeline and the connective force between vendors, guests, and the couple. With a natural ability to read a room, Maulin seamlessly adjusts in real-time to build excitement or create space. Approaching every event as a true partner, his polished and intentional stage presence ensures every announcement, transition, and highlight feels cohesive and unforgettable.

DJ Heckno is a versatile and technically precise DJ whose command of the decks translates directly into seamless dance floor energy. With a deep catalog spanning Bollywood, bhangra, hip-hop, and contemporary club music, Heckno brings a fluid, high-energy presence to every set. His intuitive ability to read the crowd and adapt on the fly makes him an invaluable member of any Revel production — delivering consistent, high-impact performances that move the dance floor from the first beat to the last.

DJ Shadow Dubai is an internationally seasoned performer whose experience spans prestigious venues and events across the Middle East, South Asia, and North America. Blending world-class technical skill with a deep appreciation for global music, Shadow Dubai brings a cinematic quality to every set — building soundscapes that are as emotive as they are electrifying. His ability to weave cultural tradition with modern production sensibilities makes him a distinctive and powerful voice in the Revel lineup.

DJ Aladdin brings a global musical perspective and magnetic stage presence to every celebration. Fluent in genres spanning South Asian classics, global hits, and contemporary fusion, Aladdin's sets balance cultural authenticity with modern energy. A crowd favorite for his seamless mixing and dynamic crowd interactions, he consistently delivers performances that move the dance floor and keep guests locked in from the first track through the final song.

Magic MC brings a rare dual specialty to Revel celebrations: interactive cocktail-hour magic and high-touch reception MC leadership. During cocktail hour, he moves seamlessly through guest groups with sophisticated close-up illusions that spark conversation, create shared moments, and set a premium tone before the formal program begins. As reception MC, he shifts into polished host mode - directing timelines, introducing key moments, coordinating with planners and vendors, and maintaining the room's energy with confidence and charm. The result is a celebration that feels both elevated and effortless, with guests engaged from the first welcome drink through the final dance-floor moment.

Production & Engineering

Every unforgettable moment is powered by a team you rarely see, but always feel.

Our lighting technicians, engineers, and production specialists are the architects of atmosphere. They translate creative vision into immersive reality, designing environments that move with the music, respond to emotion, and elevate every beat.

From intelligent lighting and LED walls to projection mapping, FX, and custom staging, our crew ensures every element is executed with precision, safety, and artistry. They handle the load-ins, cue calls, and technical logistics so you can stay in the moment.

Whether it's a candlelit ceremony or a high-energy reception, our production team adapts to the rhythm of your celebration, quietly orchestrating the magic behind the scenes.
