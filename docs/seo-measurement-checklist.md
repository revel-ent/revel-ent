# SEO Measurement Checklist

## Search Console Readiness

- Verify the `revel-ent.com` property in Google Search Console.
- Verify `www.revel-ent.com` if needed for canonical reporting.
- Confirm XML sitemap submission.
- Confirm coverage for all priority HTML pages.
- Inspect indexing for the new Atlanta intent pages and the Jayati case study.

## Sitemap Submission

- Submit [sitemap.xml](../sitemap.xml) to Search Console.
- Re-submit after major content releases.
- Confirm no unexpected 404s or blocked URLs.

## Structured Data Validation

- Test homepage Organization and Service schema.
- Test FAQPage schema on homepage and service pages.
- Test Article schema on the Jayati case study page.
- Re-run structured data checks after deploys.

## KPI Tracking

Track monthly and compare by page:

- Rankings
- Impressions
- Clicks
- CTR
- Inquiries
- Bookings
- Calls
- Portal visits
- AI search mentions

## Page-Level Priority Targets

- Homepage
- Indian Wedding DJ Atlanta
- South Asian Wedding DJ Atlanta
- Jayati + Uppal Patel wedding weekend highlight
- Client Portal

## Reporting Cadence

- Weekly internal review during launch period.
- Monthly summary after that.
- Review top landing pages, query growth, and inquiry conversion paths.

## Notes

- Capture which pages create qualified inquiries, not just traffic.
- Prioritize pages that reduce pre-call confusion and speed up booking.
- Tie new rankings back to the specific service or event phase they represent.
