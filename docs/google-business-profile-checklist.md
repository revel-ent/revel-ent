# Google Business Profile Checklist for REVEL

## Objective

Increase qualified discovery for Atlanta wedding entertainment searches and improve trust before the first inquiry.

## Recommended Primary Categories

- Wedding service
- DJ service
- Event planner or event management service, if supported by the profile scope

## Service Descriptions

Use concise, premium, specific language:

- Indian wedding DJ and MC services in Atlanta
- South Asian wedding entertainment for sangeet, baraat, ceremony, and reception flow
- Lighting and production support for premium weddings
- Planner-friendly coordination for culturally fluent event execution

## Products / Services to Add

- Indian Wedding DJ Atlanta
- South Asian Wedding DJ Atlanta
- Baraat DJ Atlanta
- Sangeet DJ Atlanta
- Indian Wedding MC Atlanta
- Wedding lighting design
- A/V and production direction
- Consultation and planning support

## Photo Categories

- Ceremony moments
- Baraat procession coverage
- Sangeet dance floor
- Reception lighting and crowd energy
- MC hosting moments
- Behind-the-scenes production
- Venue wide shots with REVEL setup visible

## Review Strategy

- Ask after the wedding weekend while the experience is fresh.
- Request reviews that mention the exact event phase and location.
- Prompt for specific proof points: communication, pacing, energy, cultural fluency, and production quality.
- Reply to every review with short, professional gratitude.

## Q&A Suggestions

- Do you provide DJ and MC services for Indian weddings in Atlanta?
- Can REVEL handle baraat and sangeet coverage?
- Do you provide lighting and production with DJ services?
- Do you travel outside Atlanta for destination weddings?
- Can you work with planners and venue teams?

## Posting Cadence

- 1 post per week minimum.
- 2-3 photo posts per month from real weddings.
- 1 short recap or event highlight every 2 weeks during peak booking season.
- Post around planning questions, venue wins, and polished event recaps.

## Profile Hygiene

- Keep business name, phone, website, and hours consistent everywhere.
- Use the same Atlanta contact info as the website.
- Update holiday or peak-season hours if they change.
- Add booking link and contact link.

## Measurement

Track monthly:

- Calls from profile
- Website clicks
- Direction requests
- Photo views
- Review growth
- Profile search impressions
