# Wedding Media Upload Spec

## Purpose

Use this structure for real-wedding photos and short-form video assets so pages can be updated quickly and consistently.

## Folder Paths

- Photos: `images/weddings/jayati-uppal/`
- Videos: `videos/weddings/jayati-uppal/`

## Naming Convention

Use lowercase, hyphen-separated slugs with a numeric suffix.

Examples:

- `jayati-uppal-hero-01.jpg`
- `jayati-uppal-sangeet-01.jpg`
- `jayati-uppal-baraat-01.jpg`
- `jayati-uppal-reception-01.jpg`
- `jayati-uppal-highlight-01.mp4`
- `jayati-uppal-baraat-01.mp4`

## Preferred Image Specs

- Format: `jpg` or `webp`
- Long edge: 2400 px preferred
- Minimum acceptable: 1600 px long edge
- Compression: aim for visually clean exports around 80-85% quality for JPEGs
- Orientation: favor landscape for story sections, portrait for detail/editorial crops

## Preferred Video Specs

- Format: `mp4`
- Codec: H.264 preferred
- Resolution: 1080p preferred
- Keep clips short and editorially usable for web embeds or highlight loops
- Trim phone clips before upload when possible

## Phone Media

- Phone photos are okay for now.
- Phone videos are okay for now.
- If possible, export or compress before upload so the files are web-friendly.
- Priority is getting the real wedding content into the repo first; optimization can happen after.

## Practical Drop-In Order

1. Upload the best 1 hero image.
2. Upload 3-5 supporting photos from sangeet, baraat, and reception.
3. Upload 1-2 short videos if available.
4. Replace placeholders on the wedding page after assets are in place.

## Notes

- Keep the same slug across page, images, and videos.
- Use the wedding couple name only in the final public page title and captions, not in every filename.
- Reuse the same structure for future real-wedding pages by swapping the slug folder name.
