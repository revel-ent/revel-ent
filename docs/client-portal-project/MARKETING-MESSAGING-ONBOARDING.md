# Marketing Messaging: Concierge Onboarding

## Positioning Narrative

REVEL helps couples plan confidently without starting from a blank spreadsheet.

Core message:

- "Select your venue, confirm guest count, and receive a production-ready baseline timeline in minutes."

## Why It Is Different

1. Atlas-powered venue intelligence is embedded from the first step.
2. Capacity and logistics checks happen before timeline decisions are locked.
3. Families can hand day-of execution to a trusted coordinator without losing control.

## Offer Ladder

1. DIY Couple
- Build timeline with Atlas safeguards
- Use concierge onboarding and guided checkpoints

2. DIY + Family Coordinator
- Pass the Baton to a designated day-of lead
- Live Mode keeps current phase and next action obvious

3. Premium Concierge
- Planner-assisted and expert review upgrades

## Sample Landing Copy

Headline:
- "Plan Your Wedding with Professional-Grade Confidence."

Subhead:
- "REVEL uses venue-specific intelligence to pre-build your timeline, surface risks early, and keep day-of execution calm."

Trust bullets:
- "Venue-aware timeline defaults"
- "Capacity and logistics checks before you commit"
- "Family Coordinator Live Mode for day-of execution"

Primary CTA:
- "Start with Your Venue"

Secondary CTA:
- "Book Expert Review"

## Tone Rules

1. Calm, direct, and premium.
2. Avoid noisy SaaS language and emoji-heavy copy.
3. Explain what to do next in one sentence.
4. Replace alarmist copy with action-oriented guidance.

## Proof Points To Track

1. Time to first usable timeline (target: < 3 minutes).
2. Onboarding completion rate from venue step to timeline approval.
3. Expert review conversion rate from "Needs Review" states.
4. Pass the Baton usage rate for family coordinator invites.
