# Operating Rhythm

## Weekly (30 min)

- Review top portal events and module click-through.
- Check consult conversion quality from portal paths.
- Review partner feedback and open risks.
- Update Delivery Checklist and Decision Log.

## Biweekly (60 min)

- Re-rank backlog by impact and effort.
- Confirm access/governance assumptions still hold.
- Validate analytics events and data quality.

## Monthly (90 min)

- KPI review and target gap analysis.
- Decide phase gate for next milestone.
- Publish summary update in execution log.

## Update Rules

- No feature starts without measurable outcome hypothesis.
- Any strategy change must be logged in Decision Log.
- Handoff doc must be updated when scope or architecture changes.
