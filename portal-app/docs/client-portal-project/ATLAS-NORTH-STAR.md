# ATLAS-NORTH-STAR

## Mission
Atlas is the wedding operating system that helps complex weddings run with fewer surprises, faster decisions, and cleaner day-of execution across couples, planners, vendors, venues, guests, and Revel operations.

## Vision
Atlas becomes the execution and intelligence backbone for premium weddings in the US, beginning with South Asian and fusion multi-day events, then expanding nationally as a standalone software business.

## Why Atlas Exists
Wedding execution is fragmented across chats, spreadsheets, memory, and role silos. High-stakes event decisions are often made with incomplete venue and logistics context. Atlas exists to turn wedding operations into a shared, role-aware, intelligence-supported system.

## The Problem Atlas Solves
- Coordination sprawl: too many channels, unclear ownership, delayed decisions.
- Hidden operational constraints: power, curfews, load-in routes, staging windows, room flips.
- Generic software mismatch: most tools optimize planning or discovery, not operational execution.
- Multi-day complexity: South Asian and fusion celebrations require parallel workflows and high-context handoffs.

## Atlas OS Definition
Atlas is an event-scoped platform combining:
- role-aware workspaces,
- execution workflows,
- and venue/logistics intelligence.

Atlas is not just a portal shell. It is a decision-support and coordination system that accumulates operational knowledge over time.

## Workspace vs Intelligence Network
### Workspace Layer
Planning, onboarding, approvals, timeline context, role-based dashboards, and day-of execution surfaces.

### Intelligence Layer
Venue Atlas constraints, capacity ranges, operational evidence, trigger evaluations, recommendations, and human override paths.

### Network Layer
Compounding cross-event knowledge from planners, vendors, venue coordinators, and outcomes that make future recommendations sharper.

## Core Value Proposition
- Couples: confidence and clarity on next decisions.
- Planners: reduced coordination overhead and earlier risk detection.
- Vendors: fewer execution surprises and better context.
- Venues: cleaner handoffs with event teams and fewer preventable issues.
- Guests: easier arrival/day-of guidance and less confusion.

## Atlas Intelligence Moat
Venue Atlas is the primary moat:
- venue constraint profiles,
- logistics metadata,
- operational evidence,
- and trigger recommendations.

The moat strengthens as Atlas captures more validated operational outcomes per wedding.

## Why Atlas Wins
- Execution-first product posture (not generic discovery).
- Role-based workflows with event-scoped security.
- Explainable, evidence-backed recommendations with override support.
- Compounding venue and operations intelligence.
- Built from domain depth in premium and South Asian/fusion wedding complexity.

## What Gets Smarter After Every Wedding
- Venue constraint accuracy and confidence levels.
- Timeline assumptions and task sequencing defaults.
- Setup/load-in/flip duration expectations.
- Role-specific risk signals and recommendation quality.
- Operational playbooks by venue/event archetype.

## Business Model Overview
Dual-mode model:
- Revel-Managed Mode: Atlas included for all stakeholders in Revel-run weddings.
- Independent Mode: paid per-wedding workspace license for non-Revel weddings.

Longer-term monetization layers:
- Vendor Pro subscription,
- Venue Pro subscription,
- enterprise/licensing opportunities.

## Revel Relationship
- Revel: premium services and execution company.
- Atlas: software platform and intelligence business.
- Brand posture: Atlas can stand independently while retaining "Atlas by Revel" trust lineage.

## Phase Roadmap Summary
### Phase 1: Foundation
- Secure role-based workspace access.
- Couple/planner core workflows.
- Initial trigger/evaluation layer for venue and operational risk.
- Clear entitlement split for Revel-managed vs independent events.

### Phase 2: Intelligence + Upsells
- Broader trigger catalog and recommendation depth.
- Better override/governance loops.
- In-workflow upsell intelligence tied to operational context.
- Early Vendor Pro and Venue Pro capability pilots.

### Phase 3: Scaling + Licensing
- National venue expansion and stronger data coverage.
- Professional subscription expansion (vendors/venues).
- Multi-account/enterprise offerings (planning firms, venue groups, white-label paths where viable).

## Success Metrics
- Decision speed: cycle time from issue detection to decision.
- Reliability: preventable day-of issue reduction.
- Workflow adoption: stakeholder activation and recurring usage per event.
- Intelligence quality: recommendation acceptance and override patterns.
- Revenue: independent workspace conversion and retention; later Pro subscription conversion.
